Strict Guidelines for Devotees

Adherence to these rules is mandatory. Failure to comply will result in denial of entry without refund.

Mandatory Traditional Dress Code

To maintain the sanctity of the temple, a strict traditional dress code is enforced at all queue entries.

For Men

  • Dhoti (Pancha) with Shirt.
  • Kurta and Pyjama.
  • Strictly NO: Jeans, T-Shirts, Shorts, Lungis.

For Women

  • Saree or Half-Saree.
  • Chudidhar with Dupatta.
  • Strictly NO: Western wear, Jeans, Leggings without long tops.

General DOs

  • Book tickets only through this official portal to avoid fraud.
  • Carry the Original Photo ID proof used during booking. Copies are rejected.
  • Report exactly 30 minutes prior to your allocated slot at the designated queue complex.
  • Maintain absolute silence, patience, and chant the Lord's name in the queues.
  • Deposit mobile phones and electronics at the designated counters before entry.
  • Respect the volunteers and security personnel.

General DON'Ts

  • Do not trust touts or middlemen. Tickets are strictly non-transferable.
  • Do not carry electronic gadgets, smartwatches, cameras, or large baggage inside.
  • Do not consume or carry alcohol, non-vegetarian food, or tobacco products.
  • Do not wear footwear inside the immediate temple premises or queue complexes.
  • Do not argue with volunteers or attempt to break the queue system.
  • Do not carry weapons or sharp objects.
